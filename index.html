📒 Cheat Sheet
🎯 Call Questions
📱 Pre-Call / Mindset
📲 Caller ID Hack: Text "Edoardo Santi at Vermeer SE" right before calling.
🧠 Mindset: I am here to ADD VALUE ("¿Cómo puedo contribuir?")
🩺 I am the Doctor: Diagnose first, prescribe second.
🐑 Sacrificial Lamb: Tell them what they don't need. Builds massive trust.
🗣️ Sell without Selling: Listen, chat, be normal. Less push = more pull.
🚪 1. The Opening
🤝 Acknowledge the Past: "..we spoke back in [XXYY] about [pain point]"
🚪 Give the "Out": "To be upfront, I don't know if this is still a priority. If it's not, just tell me. I don't want to waste your time."
🕵️ Early Recon: "Just curious, who else are you considering right now?"
📏 2. The Gap (Re-Discovery)
🩸 Current State: "How many days a month is that old machine in the shop?"
🌟 Future State: "How many more jobs are you trying to take on this year?"
🌉 Highlight the Gap: "you want to take on more jobs per week or have the ability to do more than one a day, but you're running that [old equipment], how many more jobs could you get if you were to get a [new machine]?"
⚠️ 3. Implication (Make it Hurt)
"If they cry, they buy." Expose the true cost of doing nothing.
🤔 "Does that mean...?" ...your crew is standing around on the clock?
🛑 "What happens to...?" ...your deadline if that machine goes down mid-job?
🤷 "How do you get the jobs done...?" ...if you don't upgrade and can't find operators?
📉 Loss Aversion: What will you LOSE (jobs, money, reputation) if you don't do this?
💡 4. Bridge to Value
⚙️➡️💵 Specs to Checks: NEVER pitch a feature without "...which means for you [Money/Time/Safety]."
🤝 Iron vs. Support: "You can buy cheaper iron. But when it breaks on a Tuesday at 2 PM, who answers the phone? Vermeer treats your downtime like a 5-alarm fire."
🏆 Tu éxito es mi éxito: "Your problems are my problems. I am in this for your long-term success."
🛡️ Inoculate the Competition: Tell them what the other guys will say before they hear it. "They'll say you hit the stop bar too much. But if it saves a guy's life, isn't it worth it?"
🛑 5. Objections & Ambiguity
🏷️ Isolate (Voss): "Usually it's interest rates, waiting on a bid, or praying the old rig survives. Which one?"
💰 "Too expensive!": "What makes you say that?" → SILENCE
🙅 No Blind Price Drop: "Te soy sincero — I wouldn't pay $50k-$100k without seeing it. Come in, I promise the best deal."
💸 "Lots of Money" Flip: "Is this meaningful money? YES? Good — that means you're highly motivated and I KNOW you'll be successful with it."
👎➡️👍 Negative + BUT Positive: "Yes, buying your first machine is scary, BUT you couldn't be getting it from a better company that has your back."
🔒 6. Closing & Next Steps
📅 Specific Options: NOT "When are you free?" → "Wednesday 3 PM or Thursday 11 AM?"
🤝 "Last Look" Promise: "If you go look at the competition, just promise me you'll give me a final shot. Fair?"
🏃 Take it Away: "Sounds like you aren't ready. I'm taking you off my active list. Keep my number if things change."
🔄 AAA Objection Playbook
Acknowledge → Associate → Ask
💰 Price → "I get it." + "Smart owners always check the math." → "What would the numbers need to look like?"
⏰ Timing → "Has to be right." + "Best time to build infra is before you need it." → "What milestones need to happen first?"
🤔 Think about it → "I encourage that." + "Shows you care about the outcome." → "Is it the financial side, features, or trust?"
👥 Talk to partner → "Great!" + "Unified leadership is key." → "What's their #1 skepticism about this?"
⚔️ Competition → "You should look around." + "Clients who compare stay longer." → "What's the #1 metric you're comparing?"
🏃 Too busy → "Totally hear you." + "Busiest owners benefit most." → "Too busy because things run great, or too busy fighting fires?"
😊 Happy as is → "That's great." + "Working system is half the battle." → "1-10 how thrilled with ROI? What makes it a 10?"
😤 Bad experience → "Don't blame you." + "Most clients came to fix another vendor's mess." → "What was the biggest drop-the-ball moment?"
🇪🇸 Spanish Power-Phrases
🤝 Empathy: "Ayúdeme a entender..."
💡 Value: "En base a lo que me ha comentado..."
🛑 Objection: "¿Qué te hace decir eso?"
🤝 Trust: "Te soy sincero..."
🚫 No Pressure: "No hay presión, pero sí vale mucho la pena verlo bien antes de tomar cualquier decisión."
🏆 Partnership: "Puede contar con nosotros en cada paso."
👻 Ghost Text: "¿haces Tree Service?"
🛡️ CP3: "Confianza Plus 3 — cubre la hidráulica y la electrónica para que no te preocupes."
🛡️ Vermeer Value Arsenal
🔧 Service Network: "Machines break. But when you buy from Vermeer SE, you buy 11 locations, mobile service trucks, and factory-certified techs. What is it worth to know you have a whole company rushing to get you back up?"
"Lo que agrega el mayor valor a nuestras máquinas es el servicio. Tenemos técnicos certificados y camiones de servicio listos para ir a donde estés."
🏖️ The Orlando Story: "I was on vacation at Universal. Client called at 7 AM. I called back at 8. Even on vacation, my manager and I came up with a rescue plan. Your problems are my problems, your success is my success. Nobody takes care of you like Vermeer."
"Tus problemas serán mis problemas y tu éxito, mi éxito. Nadie te atiende como Vermeer."
🛡️ CP3 "Sleep at Night": "Covers you 3 years — maintenance, hydraulics, electronics. You run the machine and make money; we worry about the rest."
"Con el CP3, estás cubierto 3 años. Tú solo trabajas y haces dinero, nosotros nos preocupamos del resto."
⚠️ Cheap Machine Trap: "The cheapest machine on the lot becomes the most expensive in the world when your 3-man crew is standing around for a week waiting on a part."
"Lo barato sale carísimo cuando tu equipo está parado perdiendo días de trabajo."
🦸 Hero Move (Loaner): "Your machine got delayed? Come sign a demo agreement — we'll loan you one so you don't miss that job. Your success comes first."
💎 Resale Value: "Fewer moving parts = lower daily costs. Vermeer holds some of the strongest resale values in the dirt industry. Long-term asset, not a disposable tool."
📝 "I don't sell iron. I sell Service, CP3 Peace of Mind, and my Cell Phone Number."
